License Plate Identification & Face Recognition

Description:

This is my Honours Project at Edinburgh Napier University. The idea of this project came from the problem, facing in Yangon; city buses were involved in most of the car accidents in the city. When it happened, 3 out of 5 bus drivers evaded and only few were arrested. Some drivers were escaped due to the lack of evidence. By doing this project, that problem can be solved and may even reduce the carelessness of the driver and improve city transportation with technology.

Algorithms:

1. OpenCV Haar Cascade Classifier (Detection of both license plate and human face)
2. Local Binary Pattern Histogram (Face Recognition)
3. PyTesseract (Plate Identification)

Results:
License Plate Identification

Face Recognition
99% - trained with 158 positive & 125 negative images
~90% - trained with 5 people with 20 images each
